Post-Appointment

Aftercare Tips

Keep your lashes looking flawless between appointments with these essential tips.

Avoid water, steam, and excessive moisture for the first 24–48 hours after application. This allows the adhesive to fully cure and ensures maximum retention.
Oil-based cleansers, makeup removers, and moisturisers can break down the lash adhesive. Use oil-free products around the eye area to maintain your extensions.
Resist the urge to touch, rub, or pull at your lashes. This can cause premature shedding and may damage your natural lashes over time.
Gently brush through your lashes each morning with a clean spoolie brush to keep them separated, fluffy, and looking their best throughout the day.
Your natural lashes shed on a cycle, taking extensions with them. Regular refill appointments keep your set looking full and fresh at all times.
