Three Pillars of Care

Our approach is grounded in co-creation, empowerment, and collaboration — working alongside you every step of the way.

Co-Create

We partner with you to develop plans that reflect your unique goals, values, and circumstances. Your voice leads every decision.

Empower

Building your capacity and confidence to navigate systems, make informed choices, and achieve lasting independence.

Collaborate

Working with your existing support network, providers, and systems to create seamless, coordinated care pathways.

Meet Our Founder

Led by Clinical Excellence

Kate Engledow founded Create Allied Health to fill a critical gap in clinical social work services. With extensive experience across hospitals, community settings, and the NDIS, Kate brings a unique depth of expertise to complex case management.

As a PhD candidate and AASW-registered social worker, Kate ensures every service is grounded in evidence-based practice, trauma-informed care, and a genuine commitment to client empowerment.
